Scanning tips

  • Hold the camera steady and make sure the label is well-lit
  • Try to capture the full front label - wine name, producer, and vintage
  • GrapeSnap works best with printed labels - handwritten labels may not scan accurately
  • An internet connection is required to scan. Browsing your saved wines works offline

Camera or location not working?

  • Go to Settings > GrapeSnap and check that Camera and Location are enabled
  • If you denied permission on first launch, you'll need to enable it manually in Settings

Deleting your data

All your wine data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, simply delete the GrapeSnap app. This removes all saved wines, photos, tasting notes, and preferences.
