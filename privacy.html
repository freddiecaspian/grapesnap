Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 March 2026

GrapeSnap ("we", "the app") is a wine label scanning app developed by Freddie Chambers. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

What we collect

Data Purpose Stored where
Device identifier Authenticate your device with our server so your scans are processed securely Device Keychain (on your phone) and our server
Wine label photos Sent to our server to identify the wine. Photos are processed and not retained on the server Your device (permanently) and our server (during processing only)
Location (GPS) Tag where you tasted a wine, so you can remember later Your device only - never sent to our server
Tasting notes Your personal notes about each wine Your device only
Wine data Name, producer, region, vintage, grape varieties identified from your scan Your device and our server (to process the scan)

What we do not collect

  • No personal information (no name, email, or account required)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No contacts, health, financial, or browsing data

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. Wine label photos are sent to our own server for processing and are not stored after the scan is complete.

Data storage and security

Your wine collection, tasting notes, photos, and location data are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Your device identifier is stored in the iOS Keychain, which is hardware-encrypted. Communication with our server uses HTTPS encryption.

Deleting your data

All local data (wines, photos, notes, preferences) is deleted when you delete the GrapeSnap app from your device. To request deletion of your device identifier from our server, email freddiecaspian@gmail.com.

Children

GrapeSnap is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content. The app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, email freddiecaspian@gmail.com
