Privacy Policy

Effective date: 31 March 2026

GrapeSnap ("we", "the app") is a wine label scanning app developed by Freddie Chambers, a sole trader based in the United Kingdom. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, who it is shared with, and your rights.

Data controller

Freddie Chambers is the data controller for all personal data processed by GrapeSnap. For any privacy queries, contact freddiecaspian@gmail.com.

What we do not collect

  • No personal information (no name, email, or account required)
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking
  • No contacts, health, financial, or browsing data
  • No cookies or web tracking

Third-party services

When you scan a wine label, the image and extracted text are sent to our backend server. If no match is found in our wine database, your server forwards the image to one of the following AI services for identification:

OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini)

Wine label images may be sent to OpenAI, L.L.C. for AI-powered wine identification. We have enabled OpenAI's Zero Data Retention (ZDR) mode. This means images and prompts are discarded immediately after processing and are not used to train OpenAI's models. OpenAI Privacy Policy.

Anthropic (Claude)

Wine label images may be sent to Anthropic PBC as an alternative AI provider for wine identification. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models. Images may be retained for up to 30 days for trust and safety purposes, after which they are deleted. Anthropic Privacy Policy.

These providers act as data processors on our behalf, processing images solely to identify wine labels. They do not receive your device identifier, location, tasting notes, or any other personal data.

Infrastructure

Our backend server runs on Railway (hosting) with a PostgreSQL database containing our wine catalogue. Anonymous device identifiers and scan counts are cached via Upstash Redis. Neither service receives personal information beyond the anonymous device UUID.

Data we do not share

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data for tracking across apps or websites
  • Location data, tasting notes, and your wine collection never leave your device

Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

We process your data under legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) - specifically, the interest in providing you with accurate wine identification when you choose to scan a label. Processing is limited to what is necessary: we only send the wine label image, and only when you actively tap the scan button.

Data storage and security

  • All data on your device is stored using iOS encrypted storage (Core Data with NSFileProtectionComplete)
  • Your device identifier is stored in the iOS Keychain, which is hardware-encrypted
  • All communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS (TLS 1.3) encryption
  • Our server database is encrypted at rest
  • Wine label images are held in memory during processing only and are never written to disk on our server

Deleting your data

Local data: All local data (wines, photos, tasting notes, location data, preferences) is permanently deleted when you uninstall GrapeSnap from your device.

Server data: Your anonymous device identifier and scan count are cached on our server and automatically expire within 35 days. To request immediate deletion, email freddiecaspian@gmail.com with the subject line "GrapeSnap Data Deletion". We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving the request.

Your rights under UK GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access - request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion - request that we delete your data from our servers
  • Rectification - request correction of inaccurate data
  • Restriction - request that we limit how we use your data
  • Portability - request your data in a portable format
  • Objection - object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email freddiecaspian@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

If GrapeSnap is available in the European Economic Area, the EU GDPR applies in addition to the UK GDPR, with equivalent rights.

Children

GrapeSnap is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content. The app is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
