GrapeSnap

Scan a wine label in seconds. Never forget a bottle again.

How it works

Tap the grape button, snap a photo of any wine label, and GrapeSnap identifies the wine in seconds. Region, grape variety, vintage, producer - all saved to your personal cellar automatically.

Your personal cellar

Add tasting notes to any wine. GrapeSnap records where and when you tasted it, building your collection over time.

Works offline

Your cellar is stored on your device. Browse your wines anytime - even in a cellar with no signal.
